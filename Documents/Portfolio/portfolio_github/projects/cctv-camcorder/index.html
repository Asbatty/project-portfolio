CCTV Camcorder

Repurposed CCTV camera housing converted into a functional handheld camcorder. Custom-machined parts, lens assembly integration, and internal electronics redesigned to fit the form factor.

Type Personal project
Date 2024 – 2025
Role Mechanical design, machining, electronics

Overview

Started with a salvaged CCTV camera housing and worked backwards from the form factor. Custom parts were machined to adapt the lens mount and integrate the recording electronics. The goal was a compact, usable camcorder that looks like something that shouldn't work but does.
